Quick tips

Small things that make a big difference.

  • Use the header arrows. Subjects scroll horizontally — tap ‹ and › to flip through them.
  • Pin questions to the chat. Keep the AI assistant open while reading a lesson and ask follow-ups as they come up.
  • Switch AI models. The chat lets you pick between Gemini and Claude — different models, different strengths.
  • Write to learn. Once a topic clicks, write a short lesson on it. Teaching is the strongest test of understanding.
